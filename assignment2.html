Corynne's Laser Cut Press Fit Construction Kit!

I started the assignment in Illustrator with a pentagon. I cut out skinny rectangles in each side. Per advice from Nadya that it would go together better if you make the beginning of the slots angled I made angles using triangles. I ended up forgetting my caliper so I just guestimated the widths of the slots. I did 2 trials with slots that were slightly too skinny and deep.

My final version the slots ended up being 0.11 inches wide and 0.657 inches long. I also decided that because I had a lot of cardboard I would just cut out as many shapes as I could.

To cut I used Joshua's recommended settings of 25 speed and 100 power and it worked perfectly for me. The first time I forgot to set the size of the document to larger than 8.5x11, but figured it out and changed the settings right away.

Overall, I was able to follow advice from Joshua and Nadya and make my kit easily without huge problems. I also discovered that laser cut carboard smells pretty bad.
